About Jones Charolais Bulls

Purebred Charolais Bulls & Breeding Cattle – Paris, Texas

Jones Charolais Bulls is a family-run operation dedicated to raising high-quality, purebred Charolais bulls and breeding cattle. Located just south of Paris, Texas, we take pride in offering cattle with exceptional genetics, disposition, and performance. Our bulls are bred for commercial and registered cattlemen alike, with a focus on growth, calving ease, and docility.

With decades of experience, Trent Jones and family are committed to honest business, customer satisfaction, and the betterment of the Charolais breed. Whether you’re looking to improve your herd or start with the best, we’re here to help.

Why Charolais?

  • Superior Growth: Charolais cattle are known for fast growth rates and impressive weaning weights.
  • Excellent Carcass Quality: Lean, high-yielding carcasses with strong market demand.
  • Adaptability: Well-suited for Texas heat and a variety of management systems.
  • Docility: Calm temperaments make handling easier for families and ranchers alike.
